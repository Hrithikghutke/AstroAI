What We Offer

OUR CORE
SERVICES

From ground-breaking to handover, Meranti Consulting Engineering delivers integrated construction and advisory services built on precision, accountability, and decades of field expertise.

Core Disciplines

ENGINEERING & CONSULTING

Structural Engineering

Comprehensive structural design, analysis, and assessment for commercial, residential, and industrial projects. Our engineers ensure every structure meets and exceeds safety standards and regulatory requirements.

  • Structural design & detailing
  • Load analysis & calculations
  • Seismic & wind assessment
  • Peer review & certification

Civil Engineering

End-to-end civil engineering solutions spanning site development, drainage, roads, and infrastructure. We handle complex terrain challenges with methodical planning and precision execution.

  • Site grading & earthworks
  • Stormwater & drainage design
  • Road & pavement engineering
  • Infrastructure planning

Project Management

Rigorous project management from feasibility to practical completion. We coordinate multidisciplinary teams, manage budgets, and enforce timelines — keeping your project on track at every phase.

  • Programme & schedule management
  • Budget control & cost reporting
  • Stakeholder communication
  • Risk management & reporting

Construction Consulting

Independent expert advisory across all construction phases. Whether you need a second opinion, dispute resolution support, or a full construction audit, our consultants bring unbiased clarity.

  • Independent expert review
  • Dispute resolution support
  • Construction audit & compliance
  • Feasibility & due diligence

Geotechnical Services

Thorough ground investigation, soil analysis, and foundation engineering to ensure your project rests on solid ground — literally. We assess and mitigate subsurface risks before they become costly problems.

  • Soil investigation & testing
  • Foundation design & recommendations
  • Slope stability analysis
  • Ground improvement solutions

Compliance & Quality Assurance

We embed compliance and quality control into every stage of your project. From regulatory approvals to on-site inspections, we ensure your build meets all standards without slowing progress.

  • Regulatory approvals & permits
  • Site inspection & QA audits
  • Non-conformance management
  • Handover & defects inspection

How We Work

OUR PROCESS

01

Discovery & Brief

We begin with a thorough consultation to understand your project goals, constraints, site conditions, and timeline expectations. No assumptions — only clarity.

02

Analysis & Design

Our engineers perform detailed analysis, modelling, and design work — producing precise documentation and drawings that leave no room for ambiguity on site.

03

Delivery & Oversight

We remain actively involved during construction — conducting site inspections, responding to RFIs, and ensuring the built outcome matches design intent precisely.

04

Handover & Support

Post-completion, we provide full documentation, certification, and ongoing technical support — ensuring your asset performs as intended over its full lifecycle.
